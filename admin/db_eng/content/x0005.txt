==Affiliate Program==
<h1 align=""center""><font color="#000000">Affiliate Program Information</font><br>

<p>You will be glad that you decided to become an Affiliate Partner!

</p>
<p>You will be bringing greater awareness about businesses in various
communities along with giving yourself the opportunity to increase your

<p>Each approved&nbsp;Partner&nbsp;receives a unique Affiliate Code.&nbsp;The code

sales. It is how you will be paid your commission.</p>
<p>The Affiliate Partner may paste&nbsp;the Affiliate Code&nbsp;on their web
-site homepage or on a bulletin board&nbsp;as many times as they want.&nbsp;</p>
<p>You will see&nbsp;this code&nbsp; hot link&nbsp; viewed&nbsp;&nbsp;on your web-site homepage

<p>The Original Affiliate Partner will recieve an additional 3% from

<p>A company check will be sent once a month to the address given during&nbsp;registration into the Affiliate Program. </p>
<p>The check is&nbsp;made payable&nbsp;to the person or company who registered into the program. They are the Affiliate Partner. </p>
